Exoplanet Statistics and Data


Exoplanet research has revealed thousands of planets orbiting stars outside our solar system,
with varying sizes, compositions, and distances from their host stars.
Detailed data gathered from space missions like Kepler and TESS provides insights into their atmospheres,
temperatures, and potential habitability.
These statistics help astronomers estimate the likelihood of Earth-like planets and the diversity of planetary systems across the galaxy.
